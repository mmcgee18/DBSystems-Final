Care Optimize

A Data-Driven Direct Primary Care (DPC) Clinic Siting Model

By: Cyron Trazona, Liam Finn, Aidan Moore, Matthew McGee

Executive Summary

Care Optimize identifies optimal locations for DPC clinic placement across U.S. counties, balancing community need against financial viability. The model produces Go / NO-GO recommendations grounded in health, geographic, and economic data to address care deserts and physician maldistribution.

Data Scope

71,507 Census Tracts

Predictive Target

Non-HPSA High Need

Simulations

1,000 Iterations/Tract

Problem Statement

The US faces a primary care crisis where HRSA designations (HPSA/MUA) are often incomplete. Care Optimize addresses this by identifying "hidden" high-need areas where a DPC model—operating outside of insurance—can remain financially sustainable while serving the uninsured.
